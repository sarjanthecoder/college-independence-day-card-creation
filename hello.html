Legacy of Freedom

A tribute to the heroes of the Indian Independence Movement.

The Struggle for a Sovereign Nation

The Indian Independence Movement was a long and arduous journey, a testament to the unwavering spirit of millions who dreamed of a free India. It was a multifaceted struggle, marked by both non-violent civil disobedience, famously led by Mahatma Gandhi, and revolutionary activities championed by figures like Subhas Chandra Bose and Bhagat Singh.

Leaders such as Jawaharlal Nehru, Sardar Vallabhbhai Patel, and Dr. B.R. Ambedkar laid the groundwork for a democratic and sovereign nation. Their collective vision was to build an India free from oppression, where every citizen had the right to liberty, equality, and justice.
